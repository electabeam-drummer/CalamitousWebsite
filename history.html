History

Formation

Calamitous was formed in late 2023 by founding members Savva Belavsky-Levitan (Drums), Francis Healy (Guitar) and Tristan Bond (Vocals) in Boyne Community School when Savva met Francis at a lunchtime concert gig with Tristan joining soon after. It wasn't long before they began to have their first rehearsals on the balcony of their own school to proffessional rehearsal studios nearby, Silent Screams was a song that was drafted at one of those rehearsals, but the song would be complete only a year later. In the meantime the band spent their time playing covers and playing various gigs in school.
